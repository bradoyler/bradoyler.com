The News Divide

Published: Nov 24, 2016 •
#projects

Concept

Take Snapshots on an hourly basis to compare home pages of the top news sites. The left, right & center columns are lightly based on research from Pew Research on the Political Polarization & Media Habits

I came across these two 'interesting' visuals that I think show particular perspectives.

Execution

Used NodeJs & PhantomJs to screen capture a collection of sites and save images to Amazon S3
